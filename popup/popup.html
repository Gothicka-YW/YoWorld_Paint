How to Use
Create Boards
  • Select a Layout (classic 3×2, banner/hero/strips/mosaic/list variants).
  • Choose Image Fit:
    • Contain (no crop)
    • Cover (fills area, may crop edges)
  • Add images: paste direct links or paste/drag & drop images into the fields.
  • Type captions (up to 160 characters) — two lines are supported with smart sizing.
  • Preview updates live and matches the exported image.
  • Click Export PNG to save your board (always 390×260).
  • To host/share:
    • Use the Home tab’s Quick Image Uploader (ImgBB) to upload the exported PNG and optionally Auto‑set, or
    • Upload to your own host and paste the link in the Home tab.

Making Art in YW

Use one of these two paths to set your Paint Board image.

Quick Image Uploader (recommended)

  1. Open the Home tab.
  2. In Quick Image Uploader: paste, drag & drop, or click to select an image.
  3. Click Upload.
  4. Optional: leave “Auto‑set as Current Image” ON to apply immediately.
  5. Enable Redirect at the top.
  6. Go to YoWorld and save your Paint Board.
  7. Turn off Enable Redirect when done.
  8. Open the paint board and press OK.

Manual host (if you prefer your own site)

  1. Resize your image to 390×260 (PNG recommended).
  2. Upload it to a host that provides a direct image link (ImgBB, Imgur, etc.).
  3. Copy the direct image link.
  4. In the Home tab, paste the link and click “Set New Image.”
  5. Enable Redirect, save your Paint Board in YoWorld, then turn Redirect off.
  6. Open the paint board and press OK.

Tips

  • Fast capture from yoworld.info: On a template, click “preview template,” then drag the preview into the URL field or screenshot (Win+Shift+S) and paste.
  • Paste everywhere: You can paste images directly into Home or Create Boards fields.
  • Clean captions: Shorter labels read better; use two lines for long names.
  • Hosting: ImgBB is integrated and reliable; set your API key in Resources.
