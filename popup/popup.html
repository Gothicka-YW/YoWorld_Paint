Making Art in YW

  1. Resize your image to 390×260 (width × height) and save as a .png.
  2. Upload image to your site of preference (i.e. imgur).
  3. Copy the direct image link.
  4. In the Home tab, paste your link into the text box.
  5. Click the “Set New Image” button.
  6. Enable the redirect option at the top.
  7. Save your paint board on YoWorld.
  8. Turn off the enable redirect option at the top.
  9. Open and re‑save the paint board on YoWorld.

Creating Sales Boards

  • Paste up to 6 image URLs into the image fields (direct links recommended).
  • Type a caption for each image — max 80 characters. (Optional)
  • Pick a Caption Font and Size from the dropdowns for consistent styling. (Optional)
  • Watch the live preview update as you paste/type.
  • Click Export PNG to save your completed board as an image.
  • Upload your image to the site of preference.
  • Copy the direct link into the Home tab, following "Making Art in YW" instructions.
⭐
Tip: In a new window, go to yoworld.info’s template → click “preview template” → now you can drag the image to the URL box. You can also Copy+Paste the text if you like!
⭐
Tip: On yoworld.info's template, use Win+Shift+S to screenshot the item card (including picture and name), then simply paste (Ctrl+V) it into a Sales Board image field in this extension.
